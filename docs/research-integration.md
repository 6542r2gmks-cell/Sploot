## Product Effects
- The homepage centers readiness and trust instead of browsing alone.
- Quiz scoring uses schedule, finances, life transitions, support depth, and expectation style as core signals.
- Appearance preference is present but intentionally down-weighted.
- Matching cards expose both reasons and cautions.
- Lifecycle support appears immediately after matching.

## Audience Effects
- Position for mainstream dog owners, not a narrow tech-forward niche.
- Keep the interface social-ready and mobile-friendly, but not reliant on trend fluency.
- Make affordability visible because cost pressure is a strong audience theme.
