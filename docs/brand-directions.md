# Sploot Brand Directions

## Warm Guide
- Tagline: `Clarity before commitment.`
- Voice: calm, reassuring, practical.
- Homepage bias: education and trust.

## Modern Matchmaker
- Tagline: `Swipe with context.`
- Voice: smart, light, accountable.
- Homepage bias: higher energy, but every interaction explains fit.

## Outcome First
- Tagline: `Better fits. Fewer failed adoptions.`
- Voice: credible, evidence-led, mission-focused.
- Homepage bias: shelter trust and outcome framing.

## Companion Coach
- Tagline: `Find the dog. Learn the relationship.`
- Voice: warm, long-horizon, support-oriented.
- Homepage bias: match plus lifecycle continuity.

## Shared Constraints
- Use high-quality dog imagery with visible eyes.
- Never let novelty positioning overpower fit and preparedness.
- Preserve a playful browse mechanic without commoditizing dogs.
- Keep affordability and real-life effort visible.
